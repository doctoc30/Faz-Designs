Our Story

Faz Designs began with a bold promise: You only pay if you love it.

Our Process

1. Discover: Quick strategy call to understand your goals.
2. Design: Live website prototype — no commitment yet.
3. Refine: Unlimited edits until you're 100% satisfied.
4. Approve & Pay: Only pay when you approve the final site.
5. Launch: We go live + provide post-launch support.
