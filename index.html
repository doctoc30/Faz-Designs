FAQs

How much are lessons?

Prices start at £30/hr for manual or automatic lessons.

Where do lessons take place?

All across London zones 1–6.

How many lessons do I need?

Depends on experience—new drivers typically need 20–40 hours.

Do you offer weekend lessons?

Yes, weekends and evenings available.

Do you provide the car for test?

Yes, car hire for test day is included.

Can I choose my instructor?

Yes, we match you with your preferred instructor.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We require 24h notice to avoid a £15 fee.

Do you offer intensive courses?

Yes, 1-week and 2-week courses available.

How do I book?

Use the quote form or contact us directly.

What age can I start?

You can start from 17 with a provisional license.
