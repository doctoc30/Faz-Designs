Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web design process and services.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Our typical timeline is 4-6 weeks from project kickoff to launch. This includes design, development, content integration, and testing. More complex projects with e-commerce functionality may take 8-10 weeks.

How much does a website cost?

+

Our custom websites start at £2,500 for basic brochure sites and go up to £10,000+ for complex e-commerce platforms. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work with businesses outside the UK?

+

Yes! While we specialize in UK local businesses, we work with clients worldwide. Our process is designed to work remotely, and we've successfully delivered projects for clients in Europe, North America, and Australia.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach. They're fully responsive and optimized for all screen sizes, from smartphones to desktop computers.

Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting and maintenance packages starting at £50/month. This includes hosting, security updates, regular backups, and minor content updates.
